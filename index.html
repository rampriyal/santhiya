Symptoms in boron deficiency of banana

Boron deficiency symptoms start on the youngest leaves of the plant. Proceeding from their margins, the leaves become chlorotic and curled inwards. Later, the chlorotic zones turn necrotic dark brown. White strips perpendicular to the veins may be seen on the underside of the lamina.

Chemical methods of Management

  • Apply 20 grams of Borox per tree.
  • Apply 10 kilograms of borox per hectare to deficient soil before planting.
